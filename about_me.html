About Me


Hi, I'm Esther, and I love Ruby.

I'm a Dev Bootcamp student and I live in Brooklyn, NY.

I did my undergraduate and master's degrees in English literature and identify as a lifetime reader (but definitely not an academic). My favorite types of books are fantasy and scifi, but I spend most of my reading time these days reading and thinking about Zen practices, mind-hacking (as in self-improvement), and minimalism.

Other than reading, there are many things I love: like making tiny art (and even trying to sell some of it), cooking, going to Renaissance faires in full costume, and spending time with my family and my dog, Ash. (To wit: Exhibit 1 is on the left.)

A quirk about me is my overwhelming love of puns. No matter how bad they might be, I always think they're just so punny.

The best thing about my life is that I'm going to spend my entire career working with code, and that feels overwhelmingly joyful. Having found something so awesome and challenging--and some days, downright magical--to which to dedicate the best of me is a gift that I didn't know I would ever recieve.
