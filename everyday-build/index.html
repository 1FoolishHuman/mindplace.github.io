Everyday Build

Building something new every day.

The Concept

I was inspired by Kerrison Garcia, who posted on Reddit a while ago about how he wanted to learn more about Rails and had decided to do so by starting a Rails Daily newsletter. He started the newsletter in the middle of a series of Rails projects, and it really impressed me: here was someone who was tackling their don't-know-enough problem simply by building more things. Lots of people talk about doing this, but it doesn't seem that many follow through.

That's where my project was born. I am challenging myself to build something small every day, or showcase a small thing I've built previously, as a way to stay on top of my learning. I'm especially going to be focusing on Ruby and Rails.
