Everyday Build


Concept

I was inspired by Kerrison Garcia, who posted on Reddit a while ago about how he wanted to learn more about Rails and had decided to do so by starting a Rails daily newsletter. He started the newsletter in the middle of his 5 Apps in 10 Weeks challenge, and it really impressed me. Here's someone who's tackling their don't-know-enough problem by just building more things.

That's where my project was born. I am challenging myself to build something small every day, or showcase a small thing I've built previously, as a way to stay on top of my learning. I'm especially going to be focusing on Ruby and Rails.
