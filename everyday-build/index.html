Everyday Build

This project was inspired by Kerrison Garcia, a Ruby on Rails developer in training. He started a Rails newsletter in the middle of his 5 Apps in 10 Weeks challenge, and it really impressed me. He was building apps in order to better his grasp of Rails, and that's exactly what I decided to do myself.

I am building something small every day: small scripts, pieces of apps, and more. My goal is to strengthen my Ruby and Rails competencies and be able to say "yep, all of these things" if someone asks me if I have experience with any of these elements of Rails development.
